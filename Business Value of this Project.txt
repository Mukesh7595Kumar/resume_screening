Business Value:

* Reduces Time-to-Hire: Drastically cuts down the manual effort of screening resumes, saving an estimated 5 minutes per resume.

* Enhances Recruiter Efficiency: AI-generated summaries help recruiters quickly understand a candidate's qualifications, speeding up decision-making.
